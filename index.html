About Me

I am a Master's student at the Institute of Acoustics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IACAS); University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Renhua Peng. Previously, I received my B.S. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Beijing University of technology in 2023.

Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of neural networks and signal processing. I focus on leveraging signal processing techniques to enhance the performance and interpretability of neural networks, with applications in speech enhancement, anomaly detection, and other domains.
